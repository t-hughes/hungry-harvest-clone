What is Recovered Produce?

20% of fruits and veggies go to waste because of silly aesthetic imperfections (they don't meet the color, shape or size standards of stores) or logistical inefficiencies (a farmer had too good of a harvest and needs to find a belly for their beloved produce). We believe that no produce should be thrown away, which is why we carefully source, hand-package, and deliver these farm-fresh and delicious items to your doorstep at a discounted rate!
